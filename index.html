ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

We are delighted to announce the Conference on Computer and Systems Engineering (CCSE) 2023, which will be held in February 8th, 2022. The 13th Conference for Computer Science and Systems Engineering (CCSE) is the annual conference of the Technical University of Ilmenau. It primarily focuses on Computer Science, Software and Hardware Engineering, IT and their applications.

And what would our conference be without the valuable scientific presentations from our international partners! Therefore, you are cordially invited to send in your original papers, unpublished research work as well as application reports for this upcoming event and to participate in CCSE 2023. Topic/Guideline

Submission of papers will be done in PDF format along with the IEEE format using Latex. The size of the paper should not be more than 10MB. Lenth of papers includinng reference is 2 pages for committee members and 4 pages for authors. Papers already published or under review by CSE or any other conference previously will be rejected.

ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

We are pleased to announce the Conference on Computers and Systems Engineering (CCSE), 2023. This year Conference is held on the 8th February, 2023. CCSE 2023 is a local-organized forum offering a platform for presenting and discussing emerging ideas and trends in Computer Science and related fields both in academia and industry-wise.

We planned for having this conference as an appealing socio-scientific event to foster the bonds between researchers and participants in our research community through enriching discussions and beneficial scientific talks. As a result of the cooperation with our colleagues and after lots of motivational teamwork.

We would like to express our appreciation to the committees members, especially the program chairs of CCSE 2023. They have put together an outstanding technical program committee that has conducted careful scrutinization during the review of submitted research to maintain the high quality of accepted entries. The successful delivery of this conference and quality publication in CCSE 2023 proceedings have also been due to the tremendous efforts of technical program committee members. We would also like to thank Prof. Zimmermann, Mr. Bedini and Ms. Giewald for their constant support and guidance throughout the whole conference preparation process.

We are eager to invite you to CCSE 2023, beside which you are cordially invited to enjoy the subsequent party.
